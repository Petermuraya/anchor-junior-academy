Importance of Pre-Primary School Activities

Imparting early education between the age of 3 and 5 is beneficial for the overall development of a child. Pre-school activities play a crucial role in laying the foundation for formal education.

Need for Pre-Primary School Activities

  • Improved Concentration: Activities boost concentration and make children good listeners.
  • Team Spirit: Group activities develop social competence and teach teamwork.
  • Inquisitiveness: Activities satisfy children's curiosity in a positive way.
  • Cognitive Skills: Practical activities strengthen cognitive skills by encouraging observation, questioning, and problem-solving.
