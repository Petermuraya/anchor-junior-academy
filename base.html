VALUES

DISCOVERY

Learning is a lifelong process. We commit ourselves to an unquenchable curiosity for discovering all that this life may offer. We recognize that we may not always find the answers, but we will continue to ask the questions.

RESPONSIBILITY

Responsibility requires that we do our best in all that we undertake. We have an obligation to ourselves to fulfill our own potentials, we have an obligation to others to fulfill the commitments that we have made, and we have an obligation to our Maker to lead lives of meaning and purpose.

EXCELLENCE

We seek excellence in all that we undertake, setting goals and achieving them, steadfast in our commitment to moral behavior. Excellence is a product of attentiveness and firmness, risk and sacrifice; we celebrate our successes and those of others.
